SUDIP CHOUDHURY
Principal Architect

SUDIP has more than 26 Years of experience in Planning, Design and Delivery of various types of large scale projects across India. He excels in senior level project coordination which includes client, consultant and vendor coordination.

A graduate in Architecture from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, he started his career under Prof Gopal Mitra (Builcon) as an Architect for various residential and commercial projects. Subsequently joined M N Dastur & Co Ltd, and worked extensively in the industrial sector in addition to the housing and commercial projects. He has also completed MBA in Finance from JU.

Joining Edifice Kolkata in 2004, he has been involved in the delivery of various large scale projects including corporate offices and IT buildings. In 2007, he moved to New Delhi to establish and head the North India operations for Edifice, where projects included commercial and hospitality projects along with large scale corporate interiors. In 2012, he moved back to native city of Kolkata to look after East India operations. In 2018, he left Edifice to start his own practice.

driven and self reliant, capable of setting aims and targets and lead by example, his collaborative approach with good interpersonal skills to engage, motivate and encourage others through change. He has been highly focused with a consistent track record of successfully delivering full lifecycle implementations to tight time scales and within budget.

At EDIFICE he has been involved in the delivery of various large-scale projects including residential and commercial buildings.
